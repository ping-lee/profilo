## 中国主导签署贸易协定，美国国际地位受挑战

        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

<Accordion allowToggle fontSize="19px">
  <AccordionItem borderStyle="none!important" mb="0.5rem">

The pact covers more of humanity — 2.2 billion people — than any previous regional free trade agreement and could help further cement China’s image as the dominant economic power in its neighborhood.
      </Box>
      <AccordionButton w="none" p={0} bg="none!important" boxShadow="none!important">
        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

<Accordion allowToggle fontSize="19px">
  <AccordionItem borderStyle="none!important" mb="0.5rem">

      <Box flex="1" textAlign="left">

To some trade experts, this new agreement shows that the rest of the world will not wait around for the United States. As other countries sign new deals, American exporters may gradually lose ground. 
      </Box>
      <AccordionButton w="none" p={0} bg="none!important" boxShadow="none!important">
        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## 用黑胶唱片重现被遗忘的精品

<Accordion allowToggle fontSize="19px">
  <AccordionItem borderStyle="none!important" mb="0.5rem">
    <Flex>
      <Box flex="1" textAlign="left">
        Around the world, artists are enjoying a resurgence of popularity thanks to the boom in reissues. Record labels and curators are increasingly devoting significant resources to re-releasing tunes from earlier decades on vinyl, CD, cassette and on digital formats, in the hope of turning them into collectors' items. 
      </Box>
      <AccordionButton w="none" p={0} bg="none!important" boxShadow="none!important">
        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

<Accordion allowToggle fontSize="19px">
  <AccordionItem borderStyle="none!important" mb="0.5rem">
    <Flex>
      <Box flex="1" textAlign="left">
        <Text as="ins">Whilst</Text> the bulk of reissues <Text as="ins">tend to be</Text> classic albums, such as the Beatles' Abbey Road, a small but growing selection is devoted to <Text as="ins">obscure cuts</Text> of funk, psychedelia and traditional music from the developing world. 
      </Box>
      <AccordionButton w="none" p={0} bg="none!important" boxShadow="none!important">
        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>


<Accordion allowToggle fontSize="19px">
  <AccordionItem borderStyle="none!important" mb="0.5rem">
    <Flex>
      <Box flex="1" textAlign="left">
        Reissues aren't a new phenomenon, but there's been an <Text as="ins">undeniable increase</Text> in recent years, amid the robust demand for vinyl. 
      </Box>
      <AccordionButton w="none" p={0} bg="none!important" boxShadow="none!important">
        <AccordionIcon />
      </AccordionButton>
    </Flex>
    <AccordionPanel px={0}>
      再版并不是一个新现象，但近年来，伴随市场对黑胶唱片的强劲需求，再版的趋势肉眼可见地在增长。
    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>

As a result, prices of limited-edition reissues have <Text as="ins">soared</Text>.

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>

    </AccordionPanel>
  </AccordionItem>
